Same content, four type systems, on the Deep Table felt. Watch how each handles three things this product leans on: instructional prose (lots of reading), the math line, and card notation + computed values. Tell me a letter.

A · Textbook

Fraunces (warm serif headings) + Inter body. Reads like a well-made textbook.
Equity & the Rule of 2-and-4

Counting your outs

Equity is your hand’s chance of winning at showdown. With Ah Kh on a Qh 8h 7c flop, any heart completes your flush — that’s nine cards still unseen.

P(flush) = P(turn) + P(river) − P(both) ≈ 36%

The Rule of 2-and-4 shortcuts this: 9 outs × 4 ≈ 36% with two streets to come.

Check Answer
Heading serif gives gravitas + warmth; Inter keeps prose crisp. Safe, classy default.
