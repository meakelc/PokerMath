## Entry 4 — 2026-05-28 — Typography: Textbook + mono numerals

Rendered 4 type systems (`.working/type-specimens-1.html`). User picked **A · Textbook** AND **mono for notation/computed values**.

- **Headings / display:** **Fraunces** (warm serif), weights 400/600, optical sizing. Textbook gravitas + warmth.

## Entry 5 — 2026-05-28 — Shape, depth, density

- **Shape:** Softly rounded. Radii ~`6-10px` on panels/buttons/inputs; playing cards use real card-corner rounding (~`8px` on a ~2:3 card). Friendly but composed.
- **Depth (tactile):** Cards and the assessment input panel **lift off the felt** with soft shadows (objects on a table). Sidebar, body text, dividers stay flat/calm. No felt texture, no gloss — physicality via shadow only, kept subtle to honor "calm study tool" + restrained motion.
- **Density:** Comfortable. Generous spacing, roomy line-height for reading-heavy instruction; reduces cognitive load.

## Entry 7 — 2026-05-28 — Assessment interaction model

- **Wrong-answer feedback: hint only.** No per-field red/green coloring. The hint appears; all fields stay neutral and editable. Maximizes productive struggle and honors non-revealing pedagogy (LDR-5). The learner re-examines the whole computation, not just a flagged box.
- **Hint logic: escalate each attempt, within the detected error.** Attempt 1 = gentle nudge; attempt 2 = more pointed; attempt 3+ = most specific steer (and may point to the relevant Cheat Sheet) — but never the answer. Escalation operates inside the *likely-error diagnosis* (e.g., the `10/50 = 20%` pot-odds denominator error, ×2-vs-×4 street multiplier, miscounted outs, ratio/percentage confusion). If the learner's error *type* changes between attempts, the diagnosis (and its ladder) switches accordingly.
- **Cheat Sheets: modal overlay.** Opening dims the felt and centers the sheet over the current screen; Esc / click-away / a close control dismisses. The Section beneath is unchanged and all in-progress assessment inputs are preserved (realizes FR-6 + the UJ-1 edge case). Resolves the PRD's carried [ASSUMPTION] toward overlay (not route change, not inline-expand).
- **On success: confirm + manual Next.** Fully-correct submission shows a distinct green success confirmation and marks the Section complete (gold ✓ in sidebar). No auto-advance — the learner reads the confirmation and clicks Next at their own pace. Calm, learner-paced, consistent with ungated navigation (FR-1, FR-13).

## Entry 8 — 2026-05-28 — Voice + input mechanics

- **Voice: warm coach.** Encouraging, human, second-person; no emoji, no hype, no cutesy. Successes affirm ("Nice — that call is +EV."); hints redirect without judgment ("Not quite — recount those outs."). Instructional prose stays plain and clear; warmth lives in feedback moments. Anchors EXPERIENCE.md Voice & Tone; brand voice register also informs DESIGN.md Brand & Style.
- **Input mechanics:**
  - **Numeric fields** (outs, streets, equity %, required-equity %): mono single-line inputs; numeric entry; `%` shown as a fixed suffix where relevant so the learner types only the number.
  - **Pot-odds ratio:** two small mono fields with a fixed `:` between them (`[5] : [1]`). Cleaner to read and validate than parsing free text.
  - **Call/Fold (LO3):** two large toggle buttons, mutually exclusive, neither pre-selected. Makes the judgment deliberate and gives the climax beat weight.
  - Check Answer validates all fields of the assessment together (FR-11); disabled until every field has input. [ASSUMPTION — confirm: button enablement requires all fields filled.]
